Best air mattresses for 2026

Quick answer

For most people, the SoundAsleep Dream Series Air Mattress is the clear top pick thanks to its reliable built-in pump and durable flocked top.

The King Koil Luxury Raised Air Mattress is a fine runner-up for those who want a taller bed, while the Intex Comfort Plush is the go-to for budget-conscious shoppers.

An air mattress can save the day when guests arrive or when you’re heading into the woods, but the wrong one leaves you waking up on a cold, sagging floor. The best models combine a quiet pump, decent insulation, and a surface that doesn’t hiss deflation mid-sleep. When choosing, focus on the height (raised beds are easier to get in and out of), the pump type (built-in is far more convenient), and the material, a flocked top helps sheets stay put. For camping, consider insulation and pack size; for home use, a plush height and sturdy seams matter most.
